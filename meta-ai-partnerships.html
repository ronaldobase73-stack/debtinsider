Meta eyes partnerships with Google and OpenAI for in‑app AI features

Aug 30, 2025 • News

Meta is weighing external AI models to accelerate features in its apps while its own next‑gen model is under development.

Leaders have reportedly discussed using Google’s Gemini and OpenAI’s models as a bridge solution. The approach mixes in‑house R&D, partnerships, and open source efforts to stay competitive.
