NVIDIA posts record Q2 FY26 revenue driven by data centers

Aug 27, 2025 • Finance

NVIDIA posted record revenue, led by data center demand tied to AI training and inference. Sequential growth in its Blackwell platform suggests continued enterprise upgrades.

Investors watched the data center segment closely; small deviations versus expectations still moved the stock, underscoring sensitivity to guidance and supply timing.
