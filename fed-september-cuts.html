Fed signals cuts could start as soon as September

Aug 28, 2025 • Finance

Fed Governor Christopher Waller signaled support for a quarter‑point cut in September, citing a cooler labor market and stable inflation trends.

Markets will parse upcoming labor and inflation prints as the FOMC approaches its September 16–17 meeting, where guidance may matter as much as the initial step.
