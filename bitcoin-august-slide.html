Bitcoin tumbles below $109K amid ETF outflows

Aug 29, 2025 • Crypto

Spot Bitcoin ETFs reportedly saw sizable outflows in August, coinciding with macro uncertainty and technical breaks, pushing BTC under $109K intramonth.

Analysts debate whether the drawdown marks a buying setup or a shift in the post‑halving pattern given increasing macro linkages.
